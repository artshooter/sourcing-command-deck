# First-pass scoring rules

Use lightweight, explainable scoring before any complex model.

## Positive signals

### Theme / style
- Exact theme hit in title: strong positive
- Multiple style tags in title/evidence: positive
- Required tags present: positive

### Fabric / detail
- Fabrics from brief appear in title/evidence: positive
- Elements from brief appear in title/evidence: positive

### Price
- Price inside band: strong positive
- Price slightly above: weak positive or neutral
- Price below: positive with caution
- Price far above: negative

### Supplier credibility
- Factory inspection shown: positive
- Shop URL present: positive
- Quantity ladder present: positive
- Higher booked count: weak positive

## Negative signals

### Forbidden / conflict
- Forbidden tag hit: strong negative
- Generic drift keywords such as costume / sleepwear / Halloween: negative

### Weak evidence
- Missing price: weak negative
- Missing shop URL: weak negative
- Missing supplier name: negative

## Profile summary

Summaries should stay factual and short. Mention:
- what matched
- price position
- credibility hints
- major risks
